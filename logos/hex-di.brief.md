# hex-di (Umbrella)

> See [LOGO-BRIEF.md](./LOGO-BRIEF.md) for shared brand context, color family, and technical specs.

## What It Is

The master package - a unified entry point re-exporting `@hex-di/core`, `@hex-di/graph`, and `@hex-di/runtime`. It IS HexDI itself. A type-safe dependency injection framework for TypeScript built on hexagonal architecture.

## Role & Metaphor

**The Complete Hexagon.** This is the brand mark. It represents the full hexagonal architecture - all six sides connected, all ports filled, the complete system.

## Key Concepts to Convey

- Completeness and unity (all three layers in one)
- Hexagonal architecture (the defining shape)
- Dependency injection (something entering from outside)
- Type safety (precision, clean geometry)

## Visual Direction

- A bold hexagon as the centerpiece
- Should feel like the "parent" logo that all others derive from
- Could incorporate the idea of a "port" - a gap or opening in the hexagon where a dependency enters
- The most iconic and simplest of all the logos

## Signature Color

`#6D28D9` (violet/purple) - the brand's primary identity color

## Designer Prompt

> Design a minimal geometric logo icon (200x200 SVG) of a hexagon with a deliberate gap on one side, and a filled circle entering through that gap - representing dependency injection into a hexagonal port. The hexagon should be an outline (stroke only, no fill) with rounded endpoints. The entering circle is solid-filled. Use a single color (#6D28D9 violet). The design should feel precise, engineered, and structural - like a technical diagram, not decorative. Must be recognizable at 32px. No text. Dark background friendly.
