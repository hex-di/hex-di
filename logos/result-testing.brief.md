# @hex-di/result-testing

> See [LOGO-BRIEF.md](./LOGO-BRIEF.md) for shared brand context, color family, and technical specs.

## What It Is

Vitest matchers and assertion helpers for testing Result/Option code: `.toBeOk()`, `.toBeErr()`, `expectOk()`, `expectSome()`, etc.

## Role & Metaphor

**The Inspection Lens over the Bifurcated Path.** Takes the Result concept and adds a testing/verification layer on top - looking inside the container to assert what's there.

## Key Concepts to Convey

- Testing/verification of Results
- Looking inside a container
- Assertion: confirming Ok or Err
- Magnification/inspection

## Visual Direction

- The Result hexagon (split dual-color) but with a magnifying glass or crosshair overlay
- Or: the Result hexagon with a circular "lens" element overlaid, suggesting inspection
- Should clearly reference the parent `result` logo while adding a testing dimension

## Signature Colors

- Base: `#10B981` (emerald) + `#F97316` (orange) from result
- Testing overlay: `#00F0FF` (cyan) for the inspection element

## Designer Prompt

> Design a minimal geometric logo icon (200x200 SVG) based on the Result logo (hexagon split into green/orange halves). Add a circular inspection element - a thin cyan (#00F0FF) circle overlaid on the hexagon like a magnifying lens or crosshair, slightly offset to one side. The circle should have a small crosshair or focus indicator inside it. Conveys: "inspecting and verifying the contents of a Result." Should be distinguishable from the base Result logo at small sizes. No text. Dark background friendly.
