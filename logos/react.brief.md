# @hex-di/react

> See [LOGO-BRIEF.md](./LOGO-BRIEF.md) for shared brand context, color family, and technical specs.

## What It Is

React integration: type-safe hooks (`usePort`, `useScope`, `useDeps`), Provider components (`ContainerProvider`, `ScopeProvider`, `LazyContainerProvider`), automatic scope lifecycle tied to React component trees.

## Role & Metaphor

**The Hex Atom.** React's iconic symbol is the atom with orbital electrons. This logo merges React's atom with HexDI's hexagon - the hexagon becomes the atom's nucleus, and React's orbital ellipses surround it. Services orbit around the container core.

## Key Concepts to Convey

- React integration (orbital/atom motif)
- Hexagonal nucleus (HexDI at the center)
- Hooks: small "electron" nodes orbiting
- Provider wrapping (the orbits contain/provide)

## Visual Direction

- A filled hexagon as the atomic nucleus
- Three intersecting elliptical orbits (React's signature)
- Small dots on the orbits (electron = hook/service)
- The hexagon nucleus could have a smaller hex inside (nested container/scope)

## Signature Color

`#0EA5E9` (sky blue) - React's color identity

## Designer Prompt

> Design a minimal geometric logo icon (200x200 SVG) of a filled sky-blue (#0EA5E9) hexagon as a central nucleus, surrounded by three intersecting elliptical orbital rings (white, thin stroke, 60-degree rotation between each). Place 3 small white dots on the orbits as "electron" markers. Inside the large hexagon, nest a smaller white hexagon outline as a "scope within a container" motif. Conveys: "React's atomic model with HexDI's hexagonal architecture at the nucleus." No text. Dark background friendly.
